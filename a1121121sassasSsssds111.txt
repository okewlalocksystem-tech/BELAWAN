							</h3>
															<div class="description">
									<p>Jurnal Abdimas Sosial, Ekonomi, dan Teknologi (ASET) with ISSN number (online <a href="https://issn.brin.go.id/terbit/detail/20220912261463539" target="_blank" rel="noopener">2962-9829</a>) is a scientific multidisciplinary journal published by the Center for Research and Community Service Trisakti School of Economics, Jakarta, and cooperate with Ikatan Akuntan Indonesia Kompartemen Akuntan Pendidik (<a href="https://drive.google.com/file/d/1AUb3b9jmScF6ybvTZYb4zViAF3So0_Wq/view?usp=sharing" target="_blank" rel="noopener">IAI KAPd</a>). ASET is a national standard journal that has a main focus on the development of science in the field of business and community service. The scope of community service includes training, marketing, local food safety, Appropriate Technology (TTG), design, community empowerment, social access, border areas, less developed areas, and education for sustainable development. Community service includes various activities for handling and managing various potentials, obstacles, challenges, and problems that exist in the community. The implementation of service activities also involves the participation of the community and partners. The service activities are arranged in an activity that aims to improve the welfare of the community. The purpose of publishing ASET is to disseminate conceptual thoughts or ideas that have been achieved in the field of community service. Starting in 2022, ASET is published twice a year, in June and December.</p>
